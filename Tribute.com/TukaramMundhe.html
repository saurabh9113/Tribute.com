IAS Tukaram Mundhe

1975-Present

"Tukaram Mundhe: The fearless IAS officer reshaping governance with integrity and unwavering dedication."

Tukaram Haribhau Mundhe, born on June 3, 1975, in Tadsonna village, Beed district, Maharashtra, is a distinguished Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of the 2005 batch, Maharashtra cadre.Early Life and Education: Mundhe's upbringing in a farming family instilled in him a strong work ethic. He pursued his education diligently, earning a Bachelor's degree in History and Social Science, followed by a Master's degree in Civil Services Journey: Demonstrating remarkable perseverance, Mundhe secured an impressive rank of 20 in the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) examination, leading to his induction into the IAS.Professional Career: Throughout his career, Mundhe has been recognized for his unwavering commitment to integrity and transparency. His steadfast approach to governance has led to frequent transfers, totaling 19 in his 18-year tenure, as he consistently challenges corruption and inefficiency.
