IAS Vikas Dhakane

1949-Present

"Vikas Dhakne: The visionary IAS officer driving urban transformation with integrity and unwavering dedication."

Vikas Dhakne, an Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of the 2008 batch, has made significant contributions to urban governance and infrastructure development in Maharashtra.Educational Background: Dhakne holds a diverse academic portfolio, including a Master of Arts in Economics, a Bachelor of Science in Agriculture, and a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.)..
Professional Career:
Indian Railway Protection Force Service (IRPFS): Dhakne began his career in the IRPFS, serving as Assistant and Divisional Safety Commissioner across various divisions, including Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Pune, and Solapur.
Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC): As Additional Commissioner, he played a pivotal role in implementing development projects, earning the corporation over 15 awards at state and national levels.
Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC): Serving as Additional Commissioner, Dhakne addressed critical infrastructure issues, such as road widening and the development of model roads.
